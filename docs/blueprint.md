# **App Name**: Synapse Growth

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Pages: Render comprehensive content for Home, Services overview, detailed AI Services, detailed Marketing Services, and Contact pages, including powerful headlines, explanatory text, benefit sections, and strategic calls-to-action.
- Contact Form Management: Provide a clean, professional contact form on the Contact page for users to submit their name, email, company, and message. Upon submission, details are collected for agency follow-up.
- Responsive Fixed Navigation: Implement a clear and responsive top navigation menu that remains fixed at the top of the viewport, ensuring easy access to all main sections across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- Interactive Service Displays: Showcase service categories on the Services page using large, elegant cards with interactive hover effects, redirecting users to their respective detailed subpages (AI Services, Marketing Services).
- AI-Powered Service Advisor: Integrate a conversational tool that leverages generative AI to analyze user-provided business needs and goals, then intelligently recommends the most suitable AI or digital marketing services offered by the agency.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant blue (#3399FF), used for prominent calls-to-action, interactive elements, and key highlights to provide electric contrast.
- Background color: Deep night blue (#142038), serving as the predominant dark background for a premium, technological aesthetic.
- Accent color: Light cyan (#99DDEB), used sparingly for secondary graphical elements, subtle adornments, and to complement the primary blue.
- Neutral color: Pure white (#FFFFFF), designated for primary text content, clear information hierarchy, and elements requiring strong contrast against the dark background.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, maintaining a professional and objective feel across all text.
- Use modern, minimalist line-art icons, predominantly in white or electric blue, to visually represent benefits and services, maintaining the sophisticated and technological aesthetic.
- Employ a responsive, grid-based layout with generous white space and clearly defined content blocks to ensure optimal visual hierarchy and a high-end, uncluttered user experience on all device sizes.
- Incorporate subtle and elegant animations, including gentle fade-in effects for content sections and luminous hover effects for buttons, ensuring smooth transitions without compromising performance.
